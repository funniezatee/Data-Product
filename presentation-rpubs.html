Project Summary and Objective

Governments are starting to believe in the idea of data sharing. Besides the fact that they do indeed collect a lot of data, sharing these data also allows transparency, opens up opportunities for collaboration, crowd sourcing of efforts, and many other possibilities.

In the shiny app, we seek to create visualizations for the Traffic Accident data from Leeds, UK in 2014.

In providing a friendlier and more interactive medium to engage with the data, this allows the laymen to better visualize from different perspectives, in turn encouraging even more people to do so.
